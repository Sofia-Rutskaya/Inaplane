About this policy

Together with our terms and conditions and cookie policy, this policy explains how we collect your information across all of our websites, mobile applications and other services and how we treat it. We will review this policy from time to time to ensure it is up to date. If we decide to change it, the most current version will be posted here. If we make significant changes, we will notify you the next time you use our services or through other communication channels.

Why and how do we use your personal data?

We use your information only when you give your consent to do so, when it is necessary to provide the services you have requested, when it is necessary to exercise or exercise legal rights or obligations, or in connection with normal business purposes similar to those described in this policy.

What personal data do we collect?

We collect, store and process certain information about each person who uses our services. But we do not collect information beyond what is necessary.

How long do we keep your personal data?

We only keep your data for as long as we need it, or for as long as we are required by law to do so. After that, we either delete them or make them anonymous so that they do not identify you. We treat data differently depending on what it is used for, but you can ask us to delete your personal data at any time.

When do third parties receive or collect your information?

We share your information only when you ask us to, when it is necessary to do business with you and provide services to you, or when we are required to do so by law. The companies you book with Skyscanner (such as airlines or hotels) will collect your data in accordance with their own policies. Sometimes companies that use our services to advertise may also collect your information (see Do we use cookies or other similar technologies? ). Finally, companies that help us deliver our services will also collect and use your data on our behalf.

How do we ensure the protection of your personal data?

Protecting your privacy is an integral part of our company culture. We do it using industry standard methods.
